================================================================================
PLAN 17 — REPLY PROCESSING
TSD Step 17: Reply Processing
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE
---------
Monitor Reply-To addresses on all subdomains, capture every reply, classify
it (positive/neutral/negative), and route accordingly. Positive replies go
to sales CRM immediately. Negative replies trigger suppression.

SOURCE COMPONENTS USED
----------------------
- cold-cli/reply detection     — In-Reply-To header matching, thread-ID fallback
- listmonk/subscriber status  — Status management on reply

IMPLEMENTATION TASKS
--------------------
1. Reply monitoring infrastructure:
   - Monitor Reply-To address on all subdomains
   - IMAP connection to each reply mailbox
   - Poll interval: every 5 minutes
   - Capture every reply — log against lead and subdomain

2. Reply detection (from cold-cli patterns):
   - Primary: In-Reply-To header matching against sent message-id
   - Fallback: thread-ID / conversation tracking
   - Stop-on-reply: configurable per campaign
   - Auto-blacklist on unsubscribe keywords

3. Reply classification (AI/LLM-powered):
   - Positive (interested): wants to talk, asks for demo, shows interest
   - Neutral (question): needs more info, asks clarifying question
   - Negative (not interested): not now, not fit, remove me
   - Unsubscribe: explicit opt-out request
   - OOO: out of office auto-reply
   - Bounce: delivery failure (should be caught by Step 15)

4. Positive reply handling:
   - Lead status = 'replied'
   - Set leads.replied_at = NOW()
   - Remove lead from cold email queue (never send again)
   - Forward to sales CRM immediately
   - Log positive reply for reporting

5. Negative reply handling:
   - Treat same as unsubscribe
   - Add to suppression_list within 1 hour
   - Reason: 'unsubscribe'
   - Lead status = 'suppressed'

6. Neutral reply handling:
   - Log reply for review
   - Do not suppress (potential future positive)
   - Flag for human follow-up
   - Do not send additional cold emails until response

7. Subdomain reply counter:
   - Increment subdomains.reply_count on every reply
   - Factor into engagement score calculation (Step 18)
   - High-reply subdomains (>5%) get priority in dispatch

8. Reply logging:
   - Full reply content stored
   - Classification result and confidence
   - Timestamp and source subdomain
   - Original email reference (message-id)

9. CRM integration:
   - Forward positive replies to sales CRM
   - Include: lead details, reply content, original email
   - API integration or webhook
   - Ensure no duplicate CRM entries

---------------

DEPENDENCIES
------------
- Plan 16 (complaint processing) — parallel feedback processing
- IMAP access to reply mailboxes
- AI/LLM for reply classification
- CRM API for positive reply forwarding

VERIFICATION
------------
- Positive reply detected → lead status=replied, forwarded to CRM
- Negative reply detected → address suppressed within 1 hour
- Reply count incremented on subdomain correctly
- Reply classification accurate (test with 50 sample replies)
- No duplicate CRM entries for same reply
- IMAP polling captures all replies within 5 minutes
