================================================================================
PLAN 4 — ENGAGEMENT SCORE CHECK
TSD Step 4: Engagement Score Check
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE
---------
Evaluate each lead's engagement history before queuing. Leads that have been
sent 2+ emails with zero engagement (no opens, no replies) are automatically
suppressed as non-engagers to protect domain reputation.

SOURCE COMPONENTS USED
----------------------
- coldoutboundskills/positive-reply-scoring  — Reply classification and scoring
- coldoutboundskills/engagement patterns     — Engagement tracking and scoring

IMPLEMENTATION TASKS
--------------------
1. Non-engager detection:
   - Query: leads WHERE send_count >= 2 AND replied_at IS NULL
     AND open_count = 0 AND engagement_score = 0
   - Auto-suppress: add to suppression_list with reason='non_engager'
   - Update lead status to 'suppressed'

2. Engagement score calculation per lead:
   - Formula: (reply_rate × 10) + (open_rate × 2)
   - reply_rate = replies_received / emails_sent
   - open_rate = opens_tracked / emails_sent
   - Score range: 0-100, updated daily

3. Per-lead engagement tracking:
   - Track opens via 1×1 pixel tracking image
   - Track replies via Reply-To monitoring
   - Track clicks via unique tracking URLs per subdomain
   - Record timestamps for each engagement event

4. Weekly non-engager sweep job:
   - Cron: every Sunday at midnight UTC
   - Find all leads with send_count >= 2, zero engagement, not yet suppressed
   - Batch suppress with reason='non_engager'
   - Log sweep results (count suppressed, total processed)

5. Engagement-based lead prioritization:
   - High engagement leads (score > 50): priority for follow-up sequences
   - Medium engagement (score 20-50): standard follow-up
   - Low engagement (score < 20): reduce send frequency

6. Engagement score persistence:
   - Store in leads.engagement_score
   - Historical tracking for trend analysis
   - Factor into subdomain engagement score (Step 18)

DEPENDENCIES
------------
- Plan 3 (suppression check) — non-engagers join suppression list
- Open tracking pixel infrastructure (1×1 image server)
- Reply-To monitoring infrastructure (Step 17)
- Redis for caching engagement scores

VERIFICATION
------------
- Lead with 3 emails sent, 0 opens, 0 replies → auto-suppressed
- Lead with 2 emails, 1 open → NOT suppressed (engagement detected)
- Lead with 1 email sent → NOT checked (needs >= 2 sends)
- Weekly sweep correctly identifies and suppresses non-engagers
- Engagement score calculated correctly per formula
