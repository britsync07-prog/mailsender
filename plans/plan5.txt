================================================================================
PLAN 5 — INDUSTRY SEGMENTATION
TSD Step 5: Industry Segmentation
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE
---------
Assign each lead to its correct industry cluster (Smart Homes, Mortgage,
Cybersecurity) and enforce complete isolation between clusters — different
domains, different IPs, different sending identities. Zero cross-contamination.

SOURCE COMPONENTS USED
----------------------
- listmonk/list segmentation       — List management, tags, subscriber grouping
- coldoutboundskills/icp-onboarding — ICP definition and qualification
- coldoutboundskills/icp-prompt-builder — AI-based ICP scoring

IMPLEMENTATION TASKS
--------------------
1. Define three industry clusters:
   - smart_homes: 17 domains (Phase 1), homeowners, contractors, installers
   - mortgage: 17 domains (Phase 1), brokers, loan officers, real estate agents
   - cybersecurity: 16 domains (Phase 1), IT managers, CISOs, CTOs

2. Lead-to-industry assignment logic:
   - Primary: industry field from import source
   - Secondary: job_title keyword matching (e.g., "mortgage broker" → mortgage)
   - Tertiary: company domain TLD/industry lookup
   - Manual override via API if needed

3. Domain pool isolation:
   - Each industry gets its own set of domains
   - No domain can serve leads from multiple industries
   - Different IPs assigned per industry cluster
   - Different sender names per industry

4. ICP qualification per industry:
   - smart_homes: decision makers with purchase authority, 10+ employees
   - mortgage: active in home lending, verified business email
   - cybersecurity: B2B decision makers at 50+ employee companies

5. Industry-tagged lead lists:
   - Create named lists per industry in the system
   - Tag leads with industry for fast filtering
   - Industry-specific pain point mapping

6. Cross-contamination prevention:
   - Validation at job creation: verify lead.industry matches domain.industry
   - Alert if mismatch detected
   - Never allow same email to be queued under different industry domains

7. Industry performance tracking:
   - Separate metrics per cluster
   - Weekly comparison reports
   - Per-industry bounce/complaint thresholds
